Gallows-tree game

  1. Guess letters to fill in the blanks before your little man gets hung out to dry.
  2. When you press "New Game", the computer choses a random word and indicates the number of letters in this word by drawing empty circles below "Word is..".
  3. Next step is to try to guess a letter. If you suggest a letter that occurs in the word, the computer fills in the blanks with that letter in the right places.
  4. If the word does not contain the suggested letter, the computer draws one element of a hangmanâ€™s gallows.
  5. As the game progresses, a segment of the gallows and of a victim is added for every suggested letter not in the word.
  6. The game ends either when the picture of the gallows and victim is fully drawn, or when you discover all letters in the hidden word.
  7. If stuck, get a hint. Good luck!
